Weekly Pedagogy

EIPM is a strategy used by code.org: Explore, Investigate, Practice, Make

Include Student Voice & Choice by encouraging students to choose WHAT project to make and HOW to make it.

PRIMM stands for Predict, Run, Investigate, Modify, Make. Try out these steps to maximize enagement and understanding for coding concepts.
